A mobile-friendly "Quản Lý Đơn Hàng" (Order Management) page for the Seller in the DNGo app. This should be a detailed view of current and past orders, matching the fresh premium style of the overall app.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
* Apply a "Fresh & Clean Dashboard" aesthetic.
* Use `#26CD3A` as the primary accent color, with `#F5F9F6` as the overall app background.
* Ensure all cards and containers are white `#FFFFFF` with `16px` border-radius and very faint drop shadows (5% opacity).

**Page Structure:**
1. Header: Title "Quản Lý Đơn Hàng", a Search bar, and Filter chips (Tất cả, Chờ xử lý, Đang giao, Đã hoàn thành, Đã hủy).
2. Order List: Card-based list where each card shows:
   - Order Status (e.g. Chờ xử lý in an orange pill, Đã hoàn thành in a green pill).
   - Order ID and Time.
   - Summary of items (e.g. "2x bó rau muống, 1x kg cà chua...").
   - Total price.
   - Actions: Primary button "Xác nhận đơn" or "Giao hàng", Secondary button "Chi tiết".
